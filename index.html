Our Company History

Best Georgian Wines started in 1908 in the heart of Kakheti, with a single family qvevri cellar. Over decades we have preserved old-world Georgian techniques, expanded production, and now supply premium wines worldwide.

Our story is rooted in generations of winemakers, community vineyards, and a mission to deliver authentic Georgian taste with modern quality assurance.

About Best Georgian Wines

Best Georgian Wines brings authentic Georgian winemaking to global tables. We focus on balanced reds and aromatic whites with historic roots.

Our History

Best Georgian Wines started in 1908 as a family winery in Kakheti, committed to traditional qvevri winemaking and strong local heritage. Since the 1920s we have expanded to export markets through generations, marrying modern standards with Georgian terroir.

Today, we offer a curated collection across red, white, amber and sparkling Georgian varieties with a transparent supply chain and purpose-built customer support.
